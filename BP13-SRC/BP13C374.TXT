       1000-OPEN-FILE.                                                          
      *--------------------------------------------------------*                
                                                                                
           OPEN INPUT  BP13F121                                                 
                       BP13F671                                                 
                       SY02F001                                                 
                I-O    BP13K673                                                 
                OUTPUT P13L374A                                                 
                       P13L374B                                                 
                       P13L374C                                                 
                       BP13F673.                                                
                                                                                
           EVALUATE WS-K673-STATUS                                              
              WHEN 00                                                           
              WHEN 97                                                           
                   CONTINUE                                                     
              WHEN OTHER                                                        
                   MOVE WS-K673-STATUS      TO RETURN-CODE                      
                   DISPLAY 'OPEN BP13K673 FAIL. STATUS=' WS-K673-STATUS         
                   PERFORM 9000-CLOSE-ROUTINE THRU 9000-EXIT

       1000-EXIT.
